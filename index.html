Magical Me

Madison Smith

Full-time World Traveler

In a world where wanderlust knows no bounds, one young woman is breaking the mold and redefining the way we think about travel. Meet Maddie, a fearless globetrotter who has turned her passion for exploring the world into a full-time career. From the lush jungles of the Amazon to the bustling streets of Tokyo, Maddie has made it her mission to traverse the globe, one destination at a time. At the tender age of 21, she has already visited 30 countries on 4 different continents, accumulating a wealth of cultural experiences that most people can only dream of.

Maddie's journey first began when she was little during family vacations, where she discovered her deep-rooted passion for exploring new places and cultures.

Growing up, Maddie's family prioritized travel as an integral part of their lives. These trips took her to places ranging from the bright blue waters in Cancun, Mexico to old, enchanting streets of Rome, Italy. Each destination left an indelible mark on her heart and ignited her curiosity about the world. It was during these formative years that Maddie realized that her life's purpose lay in travel.

One of her favorite trips was her Study Abroad in Spain. It was her very first big solo trip that she had taken. She visited big cities like Madrid and Barcelona and smaller towns that most have never heard of.

Travel Vlogger

Maddie is not your average world traveler. At 21 years old, she has already transformed her love for travel into a full-time career and global phenomenon. Her captivating travel vlog, Beyond Borders, has helped fund her adventures due to her high following of over 10 million people. On her travels, she has learned 5 languages, met world leaders, and formed deep connections with indigenous tribes. Her content offers viewers a front-row seat to the diverse cultures, breathtaking landscapes, and extraordinary encounters she experiences during her travels.

Maddie's videos from Norway were the most popular amongst viewers leaving them spellbound. From the breathtaking fjords to the mesmerizing Northern Lights, her videos have beautifully showcased the natural wonders of Norway.

On her Germany trip, Maddie's videos reflected European culture, capturing the vibrant cities, historic landmarks, and delectable cuisine. Her "Octoberfest" episode was also a hit, due to the fun and lively videos filmed inside the tents along with her advice for people planning on going in the future.

Spain holds a special place in Maddie's heart though. By making friends with the locals, her travel vlogs reveal the country's rich heritage and diverse landscapes through a different perspective. Her exploration of Spanish festivals and traditions resonated with viewers, offering an immersive experience of the culture.

63 National Parks

In an extraordinary journey of exploration and adventure, Maddie, a determined and intrepid solo traveler, has accomplished a remarkable feat by visiting all 63 United States National Parks. Her inspiring odyssey has captivated audiences around the world and has solidified her status as a leading travel vlogger.

Maddie's epic quest began 2 years ago when she decided to embark on a mission to explore the natural wonders of the United States. Armed with little more than her backpack, camera, and unyielding passion for the great outdoors, she set out to experience the beauty, diversity, and ecological significance of every national park in the country.

She traversed a wide range of landscapes, from the rugged peaks of Denali in Alaska to the serene marshes of the Everglades in Florida. Her awe-inspiring vlogs and vivid storytelling have allowed viewers to vicariously experience the grandeur of these national treasures, inspiring countless individuals to embark on their own adventures.

"Visiting all 63 national parks was a dream come true, and I hope my experiences encourage others to explore and protect these invaluable landscapes."

Maddie's journey was not without its challenges, from confronting unpredictable weather conditions to mastering the art of solo travel. Her unwavering determination and resilience have set an example for young women and aspiring adventurers everywhere, proving that dreams are attainable with dedication and perseverance.

Follow Maddie's ongoing adventures and conservation efforts on her popular vlog, Beyond Borders, and prepare to be inspired by her remarkable journeys and dedication to preserving the United States' natural treasures.
